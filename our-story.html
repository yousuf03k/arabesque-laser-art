A Family Business

Arabesque Laser Art was built on the fundamental principle of architecture that the resident should be at the center of design in any dwelling. The belief is that every design should be functional, private, and add beauty to the space that it is in.

Our designs have a variety of decorative and practical uses. Our machines can create patterns on wood, acrylic, glass, plastic, aluminum brass, steel, leather, and fabrics. From bedrooms to kitchens to conference rooms and everywhere in between, interlaced geometric patterns and foliage will enhance any space.

Using our CNC and Laser machines, we can also create custom railings for stairs and covers for terraces. We can help you decorate wooden facades, doors, windows and house fronts. It is easy with these designs to create privacy without disconnecting you from your environment.

This team of experts brings years of experience and wants to help you redesign your space's interior to create better harmony between you and your surroundings. We aim to help you create an enjoyable, comfortable, and personalized environment.
